Our History

Clackamas United has proudly served the Happy Valley, Clackamas, and surrounding communities for over 15 years, providing a welcoming place for young players to learn, play, and grow through the game of soccer.

Our mission is to create a fun, safe, and inclusive environment for players of all abilities, where they can develop their skills, build confidence, and foster a lifelong love for the game. We are committed to promoting teamwork, sportsmanship, and personal growth, while supporting players both on and off the field and building a strong sense of community within our club.
