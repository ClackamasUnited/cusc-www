Scholarship Program Updates

By Tony Stevenson

An update on our scholarship program and the application process for the upcoming season.

As we prepare for the new season, we want to provide an update on our scholarship program. We are committed to providing financial assistance to as many families as possible. The application process is integrated into our registration system. Please read the instructions carefully on our scholarships page before applying. We look forward to another successful year of making soccer accessible to all.
