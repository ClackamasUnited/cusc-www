2025/26 Classic Season Tryout Schedule

By Tony Stevenson

The full schedule for our competitive team tryouts starting in May is now available.

Get ready for the new season! We have finalized the tryout schedule for our 2025/26 Classic (competitive) teams. All players interested in joining a competitive team must attend the tryouts for their age group. Please check the schedule carefully for dates, times, and locations. Registration is required prior to attending.
