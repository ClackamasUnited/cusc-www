Happy Valley Fun Run 2025

By Tony Stevenson

Join us for the Happy Valley Fun Run! A great community event for all ages.

Clackamas United is proud to support the Happy Valley Fun Run. It's a fantastic opportunity for our players, families, and the entire community to come together for a healthy and enjoyable event. We encourage everyone to participate and show their community spirit. See you at the starting line!
