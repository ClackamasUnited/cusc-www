Scholarship Program Update

By Tony Stevenson

A look back at the $56,000+ awarded in financial aid during the 2024/25 season.

We are proud to share the results of our scholarship program for the past season. The club successfully distributed over $56,000 in financial aid, helping numerous families in our community. This initiative is at the core of our mission to make soccer accessible for all. We are now preparing for the next application cycle and encourage interested families to review the guidelines on our website.
