2024-25 Scholarship Program Summary

By Tony Stevenson

A summary of the 2024-25 season's scholarship program, which provided over $55,000 in aid to 169 families.

Looking back on the 2024-25 season, we are thrilled to report the significant impact of our scholarship program. By providing substantial financial aid, we were able to support a large number of players and families, ensuring that the joy of soccer is accessible to all. Thank you to everyone who contributed to this success. For more information on our scholarship program, please visit our scholarships page.
