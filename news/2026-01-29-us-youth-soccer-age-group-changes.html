US Youth Soccer Age Group Changes

By Tony Stevenson

A guide to the nationwide changes coming in the 2026/27 Soccer Season regarding age group alignments.

US Youth Soccer has announced a nationwide shift to align age groups with the school year calendar, starting with the 2026-27 season. This change is intended to allow players to compete with their classmates, simplifying team formation and promoting social development. We understand this is a significant change and will be providing more information and resources to our families as we prepare for the transition.
