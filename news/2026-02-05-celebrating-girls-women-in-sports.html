Celebrating Girls & Women in Sports

By Tony Stevenson

Today we celebrate not just our talented players, but also our incredible female coaching staff who inspire, mentor, and lead the next generation.

At Clackamas United, we are proud to champion the involvement of girls and women at every level of the game. From our youngest players in the recreational leagues to our competitive teams and dedicated coaches, their passion and dedication are the heartbeat of our club. Join us in celebrating their game, their future, and their impact on our community.
