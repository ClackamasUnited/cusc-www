Scholarship Program Updates

Feb 18, 2026 by Tony Stevenson


Clackamas United Soccer Club is committed to making soccer accessible to all youth in our community, regardless of their financial circumstances. We believe that every child deserves the opportunity to play, learn, and grow through the beautiful game.

Our scholarship program is designed to provide financial assistance to families who demonstrate a clear need, helping to cover registration fees for our recreational and competitive programs.

How to Apply

Applying for a scholarship is a straightforward process. Families interested in financial aid should:

  1. Complete the online scholarship application form, available on our website under the "Registration" section.
  2. Provide proof of income or other documentation demonstrating financial need (e.g., eligibility for free or reduced lunch programs, recent tax returns, or other relevant financial statements).
  3. Submit all required documents by the application deadline for the upcoming season to ensure timely review.

Important Dates

  • Spring Season 2026: Scholarship applications open January 15th, 2026 and close February 28th, 2026.
  • Fall Season 2026: Scholarship applications open June 1st, 2026 and close July 15th, 2026.

For more detailed information about our scholarship program, including specific eligibility requirements and a list of frequently asked questions, please visit our official Scholarship Program page or contact our club administrator directly at info@clackamasunited.com.
