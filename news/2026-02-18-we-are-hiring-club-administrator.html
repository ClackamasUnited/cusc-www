We're Hiring: Club Administrator

Feb 18, 2026 by Tony Stevenson

Clackamas United Soccer Club (CUSC) is seeking an organized and dedicated Club Administrator to support our club's operations and ensure a smooth experience for our members. This is a key role for a detail-oriented professional who is passionate about youth sports.

About the Role

The Club Administrator will be responsible for managing the day-to-day administrative tasks of the club. This role involves handling member communications, managing registrations, coordinating schedules, and providing support to the board and coaching staff.

Key Responsibilities

  • Manage player registration and payment processes.
  • Serve as the primary point of contact for member inquiries.
  • Coordinate field and game scheduling with leagues and facilities.
  • Maintain club records and manage administrative databases.
  • Provide administrative support for club events, tryouts, and meetings.

How to Apply

Interested candidates should submit a resume and cover letter to president@clackamasunited.com. Applications will be reviewed on a rolling basis until the position is filled.
