2025-26 Scholarship Program Summary

By Tony Stevenson

Clackamas United is proud to have awarded over $56,000 in financial aid to 85 families for the 2025-26 season.

Our commitment to making soccer accessible to everyone in our community remains a top priority. This year's scholarship program demonstrates our dedication to ensuring that financial barriers do not prevent young athletes from participating. We are incredibly grateful to our donors and sponsors who make this possible. For more information on our scholarship program, please visit our scholarships page.
