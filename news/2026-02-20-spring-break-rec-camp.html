Spring Break Rec Camp

By Tony Stevenson

Registration is now open for our annual Spring Break Recreational Camp! A fun-filled week of soccer for all skill levels.

Join us from March 23rd to 26th at Clackamas High School Stadium for a fantastic week of soccer. The camp is designed for players of all abilities to improve their skills, make new friends, and have a great time. The camp runs from Monday to Thursday, with sessions for different age groups. Don't miss out on the fun!
