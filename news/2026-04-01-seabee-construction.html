New Sponsor Seabee Construction

By Tony Stevenson

Clackamas United Soccer Club Announces Seabee Construction as New Title Sponsor

CLACKAMAS, OR — March 24, 2026 — Clackamas United Soccer Club is thrilled to announce a landmark partnership with Seabee Construction, who will officially become the club’s new Title Sponsor effective April 1st, 2026. This collaboration marks a significant milestone for youth soccer in the Clackamas and Happy Valley areas, uniting two organizations deeply committed to the growth, well-being, and spirit of our local community.


A Foundation Built on Community

For Clackamas United, soccer has always been about more than just the game; it’s about providing a fun, safe, and engaging environment for our young players to develop life skills. By partnering with Seabee Construction, we are aligning with a local, family-owned business that understands the value of hard work and dedication. As a Veteran-owned company, Seabee Construction brings a unique perspective on leadership and service that resonates perfectly with our mission to foster excellence in our youth athletes.

"We are beyond grateful to welcome Seabee Construction to the Clackamas United family," said the Club Board of Directors.

"Their commitment to supporting grassroots sports is a testament to their investment in the future of Happy Valley and Clackamas. This partnership ensures that we can continue to provide high-quality soccer programs while keeping the focus on community and fun."

Supporting the Next Generation

Seabee Construction’s involvement goes far beyond a logo on a jersey. Their support will directly impact the resources available to our young players, directly supporting our clubs' scholarship program, and more. As a business that prides itself on being "built local," Seabee Construction’s decision to champion grassroots sports highlights their belief that the strongest communities are built from the ground up—starting with our children.

Looking Ahead

Starting this April, families and fans will see the Seabee Construction name integrated into the Clackamas United experience. This partnership represents a mutual "thank you" to the families who make this club possible and a promise to continue investing in the local talent that makes our region so vibrant. We invite the entire community to join us in celebrating this exciting new chapter as we hit the pitch for the Spring season.


About Clackamas United Soccer Club:

Clackamas United is a premier youth soccer organization serving the Clackamas and Happy Valley communities. We focus on player development, sportsmanship, and fostering a lifelong love for the game of soccer.


About Seabee Construction:

Seabee Construction is a Veteran-owned, locally operated business specializing in high-quality construction services for various federal agencies. They are dedicated to building a stronger community through excellence in craftsmanship and a commitment to local philanthropic initiatives.
