Join us this summer for Clackamas United Rec Camps, where players can build skills, make friends, and enjoy the game in a fun and positive environment!


Our camps are designed to focus on individual skill development and small-sided games, helping players grow their confidence while having a great time on the field. Whether your player is brand new to soccer or already loves the game, our coaches create an atmosphere that is encouraging, energetic, and age-appropriate.


New this year: We’re excited to introduce a 3–4 year old age group, giving our youngest players a chance to learn the basics through fun, engaging activities designed just for them!


📍Locations: Some camp locations are currently listed as TBD and will take place within the North Clackamas area. We are currently waiting for schools and parks to open their reservation systems and we will notify families as soon as locations are confirmed.
