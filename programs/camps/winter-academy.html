Winter Academy

November - January

It's cold and wet, but it's time for our fun and skill-focused Winter Academy! This program is designed for players who want to continue developing their technical skills during the winter months.

Our Winter Academy program includes 10 training sessions throughout December and January; with no sessions scheduled during the winter break.

Program Details

The Winter Academy focuses on individual ball mastery, small-sided games, and technical repetition in a fun, high-energy environment. It's the perfect way to stay sharp and get ready for the spring season.

  • Focus on technical skills and footwork.
  • Small-sided games to encourage quick decision-making.
  • Professional coaching staff.
  • Open to players of all skill levels within the specified age groups.
