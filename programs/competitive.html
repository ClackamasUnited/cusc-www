Competitive Program

Our Classic program is for players in 5th - 12th grade.


Our Classic Program is designed for those 5th–12th grade players seeking a competitive soccer experience with a strong focus on development, commitment, and team culture. This program builds on foundational skills and prepares players to compete at the high school level and beyond. Teams train twice per week and compete in structured seasonal play.

Players in 5th–8th grade participate in both Fall (10 games plus a Fall Cup competition) and Spring (8 games) seasons, while our High school aged players (9th–12th grade) compete in a Winter season that includes a 10-game schedule and a Cup competition.

Beyond competition, our Classic Program emphasizes growth, teamwork, and creating a positive environment where players can build confidence, develop leadership skills, and form lifelong friendships.

Program at a Glance
