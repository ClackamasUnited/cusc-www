Competitive Program

Our Classic program is for players in 5th - 12th grade.

  • The program is aimed at players focused on learning and playing soccer in a fun, competitive and highly educational environment.
  • Geared towards developing talented players able to compete in high school soccer.
  • Players in the 15-18 year old group develop and have the opportunity to play soccer at high school.
  • This is the final chapter of their youth soccer experience, and is an important stepping stone into the adult game.

Program at a Glance

Ages: 5th Grade - 12th Grade
Level: Competitive
Season: Year-round (Fall & Spring)
Training: 2 times per week
Coaching: Licensed, professional coaches
Focus: Advanced skill & tactical development
