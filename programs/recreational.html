Recreational Program

Our Recreational Program is designed to provide a fun, supportive environment where players can learn the game, build confidence, and develop a love for soccer. Open to players from Pre-K through 8th grade, the program focuses on skill development, teamwork, and enjoyment at every level.

Fall Season

  • For players in Kindergarten - 8th Grade.
  • Season runs from late August to late October.
  • Practices are typically once a week.
  • Games are held on Saturdays.

Spring Season

  • For players in Kindergarten - 8th Grade.
  • Season runs from early April to late May.
  • Practices are typically once a week.
  • Games are held on Saturdays.

Want to Coach ?

Our recreational program is made possible by dedicated volunteer coaches who play a vital role in creating a positive and encouraging experience for every player. These coaches help teach the fundamentals of the game, foster teamwork, and ensure that every child feels included and supported.


If you are interested in becoming a volunteer coach, we would love to have you involved! Please contact our Club Registrar using the information at the bottom of this page.

Program Breakdown

PreK - 1st Grade

Players practice once per week and participate in one game during the week. PreK/K games are typically held on Tuesdays, while 1st grade games are usually on Thursdays.

These age groups are coed and emphasize introduction to the game through fun, engaging activities.

Register for Pre-K - 1st Grade → Registration Closed

2nd Grade

2nd grade is an in-house program where teams practice twice per week and play games on Saturdays against other 2nd grade teams.

During the fall season, teams are separated by gender, while the spring season is coed.


View the game schedule for the current season


Register for 2nd Grade → Registration Closed

3rd - 8th Grade

Teams compete in the Soccer 5 League (link), practicing twice per week and playing games on Saturdays.

The fall season consists of 8 games, while the spring season typically includes 7 games.

All practices take place at local fields throughout North Clackamas and Happy Valley.
