Recreational Program

The heart of our club, our recreational program is open to all players from Kindergarten to 8th grade, regardless of experience. The focus is on fun, participation, and learning the basics of soccer.

Fall Season

  • For players in Kindergarten - 8th Grade.
  • Season runs from late August to late October.
  • Practices are typically once a week.
  • Games are held on Saturdays.
