Rangers FC Affiliation

Our official partnership with Rangers Football Club of Glasgow, Scotland.

A Partnership for Excellence

Clackamas United is proud to be an official partner club of Rangers Football Club, one of Scotland's most historic and successful professional teams. This affiliation provides our players and coaches with unparalleled access to the Rangers coaching curriculum, methodology, and professional development opportunities.

Through this partnership, our players have unique opportunities to attend Rangers ID camps and receive training from Rangers Academy coaches, while our own coaching staff benefits from continuous education and support from a world-class organization.
