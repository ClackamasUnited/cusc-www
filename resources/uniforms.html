Uniform Policy

Information on uniform requirements for all Clackamas United soccer programs.

Rec Uniforms

Pre-K - 1st Grade

Game shirts will be provided. Players should wear comfortable athletic clothing that allows them to move freely during practice. Shinguards and cleats are also required.

No purchase necessary.

2nd - 8th Grade

Rec uniforms can be picked up in-store or ordered online through Tursi (using the button below).

Required items include:

  • Nike red top
  • Nike black top
  • Nike black shorts
  • Nike black socks

Players should wear comfortable athletic clothing that allows them to move freely during practice. Shinguards and cleats are also required.
