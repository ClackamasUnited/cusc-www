Uniform Policies | Clackamas United

Information on uniform requirements for all Clackamas United soccer programs.

Recreational Program

For our recreational players (Kindergarten through 8th Grade), the club provides a team jersey as part of the registration fee. Parents are responsible for providing:

  • Black soccer shorts
  • Red soccer socks
  • Shin guards (required for all practices and games)
  • Soccer cleats

Jerseys are distributed by the team coach before the first game of the season.

Competitive Program

Players in our competitive program are required to purchase the official Clackamas United uniform kit. The kit includes home and away jerseys, shorts, and socks. Uniforms are ordered through our official supplier, Tursi's Soccer.

Once a player is assigned to a team, they will receive an email with a link and instructions for ordering their kit online. It is crucial to order the kit promptly to ensure it arrives before the first match of the season.
