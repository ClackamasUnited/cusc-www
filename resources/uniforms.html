Uniform Policy

Information on uniform requirements for all Clackamas United soccer programs.

Rec Uniforms

Pre-K - 1st Grade

Game shirts will be provided. Players should wear comfortable athletic clothing that allows them to move freely during practice. Shinguards and cleats are also required.

No purchase neccesary.
