Scholarship Program

Clackamas United is committed to making soccer accessible to all youth players in our community, regardless of financial circumstances.

We believe that every child deserves the opportunity to play, learn, and grow through the beautiful game. Our scholarship program is designed to provide financial assistance to families who demonstrate a clear need, helping to cover registration fees for our programs, and uniforms.

How to Apply

Applying for a scholarship is a straightforward process. Families interested in financial aid should:

  • Complete the online Financial Aid application form within Playmetrics during the registration process.*
  • Complete the questionnaire in the application.
    • DO NOT complete registration yet. *
    • DO NOT make a payment until a scholarship decision has been communicated. *

For more detailed information, please contact our club administrator directly at scholarships@clackamasunited.com.

*N.B. Please note that you will not lose a place on a team while an open financial aid request is pending.

What information do I need to provide?

All applications must be submitted with accompanying documentation that demonstrates the household income. This will typically be a copy of your US Tax Return 1040 Cover Sheet.

  • YOU MUST redact all social security numbers and any other sensitive information from your cover sheet.
  • DO NOT send your complete tax return.
  • After April 1st YOU MUST submit the cover sheet from the last tax year. If you do not have a submitted tax return, use the prior year with a copy of an extension request.
  • For example, if the date is:
    • January 1st - April 15th 2026 - submit your tax return from 2025 (if you have submitted it) otherwise use 2024.
    • April 16th - December 31st 2026 - submit your tax return from 2025, (or 2024 with an extenstion request).
